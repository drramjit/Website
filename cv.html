Profile

Dr. Ramjit Kumar

Assistant Professor of Sociology specialising in agrarian studies, caste, and social stratification in rural India. Sixteen years of fieldwork in the eastern Gangetic plain, three authored books, and a sustained record of peer-reviewed publication and doctoral supervision.

Selected publications

Three books and 42 peer-reviewed papers. Recent highlights:

  • Soil and Status: An Agrarian Sociology of North Bihar (Cambridge University Press, 2024).

  • "Circular Migration and Household Authority," Journal of Agrarian Change (2024).
  • "The Quiet Tenant," Modern Asian Studies (2022).
