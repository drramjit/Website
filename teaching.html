Teaching

Teaching interests

I approach teaching as an extension of my research: helping students see science, society, and knowledge itself as objects of careful sociological enquiry.

Philosophy

Teaching as epistemology in practice

For me, the classroom is where the nature of knowledge is negotiated, not just delivered. Whether teaching social theory or research methods, I want students to ask not only what we know but how we come to know it — and to carry that critical, reflexive habit into their own work. My dual training in interpretive and quantitative traditions lets me move students between the rigour of measurement and the depth of meaning.

For students
